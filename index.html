How to Use This App

  1. Map Tab: Shows all 9 sampling points. Tap a point to see details.
  2. Navigate: Opens Google Maps directions to the selected point.
  3. Take Sample: Shows step-by-step sampling checklist.
  4. Record Data: Opens form to record soil observations.
  5. Progress dots: Track completed points at bottom of map.

    At Each Point

    1. Clear surface debris (leaves, stones)
    2. Dig a V-shaped cut to 15 cm depth
    3. Take a vertical slice from the cut face
    4. Collect approximately 500g of soil
    5. Place in labeled bag
    6. Record field observations in the app

    Bag Labeling

    Write on each bag:

    • Sample Number: 1-9
    • Grid Reference: e.g., G5
    • Date: DD/MM/YYYY
